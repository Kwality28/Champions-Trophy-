What is the Champions Trophy?

  • Inaugurated in 1998, The ICC conceived the idea of the Champions Trophy â€“ a short cricket tournament to raise funds for the development of the game in non-test playing countries.

  • Since 2002, the tournament has been held in full ICC member nations with the number of teams reduced to eight. The tournament, later dubbed the "Mini World Cup" as it involved all of the full members of the ICC

Where is it this year?

  • The tournament runs from 19 February to 9 March and will take place in Pakistan and the United Arab Emirates. It is the first ICC global event in Pakistan since 1996 due to safety concerns after a terrorist attack in 2009.

  • India won a famous grand finale against New Zealand in the Champions Trophy 2025 at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium on Sunday, March 09

Which teams are in it?

  • Group A features Bangladesh, India, Pakistan, and New Zealand, while Group B includes Afghanistan, England, Australia, and South Africa.

  • The tournament opener will see the home team, Pakistan, face New Zealand on February 19th in Karachi.

Which team has won the most?

  • As of now, ahead of the 2025 edition in Pakistan and Dubai, India holds the record of most wins in the ICC Champions Trophy with 18 wins in 29 matches.

  • The next joint-best are England and Sri Lanka with 14 each in 25 and 27 matches respectively.
